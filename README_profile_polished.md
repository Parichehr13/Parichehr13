## About Me

Biomedical engineer with a background in bioelectric engineering, currently pursuing an MSc in **Biomedical Engineering for Neuroscience** at the **University of Bologna**.

My work sits at the intersection of **EEG signal processing**, **biomedical data analysis**, **machine learning**, and **computational neuroscience**. I enjoy building projects around **EEG preprocessing and decoding**, **brain signal analysis**, and **medical image analysis**, with a growing focus on using **AI and quantitative methods** to better understand **brain signals** and **neural processes**.

I’m especially interested in developing computational approaches for **neuroscience research** and **healthcare applications**.
