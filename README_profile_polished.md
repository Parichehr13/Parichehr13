## About Me

I’m interested in the space where **brain signals**, **computation**, and **intelligent systems** come together, and I’m currently completing an MSc in **Biomedical Engineering for Neuroscience** at the **University of Bologna**.

My work revolves around **EEG signal processing**, **biomedical data analysis**, **machine learning**, and **computational neuroscience**. I enjoy building projects around **EEG preprocessing and decoding**, **brain signal analysis**, and **medical image analysis**.

More broadly, I’m curious about how **AI** and **quantitative methods** can be used to study **neural processes**, explore complex biological data, and support **neuroscience research** and **healthcare applications**.
