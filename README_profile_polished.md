## About Me

I like working where **brain signals**, **models**, and **code** meet, and I’m currently completing an MSc in **Biomedical Engineering for Neuroscience** at the **University of Bologna**.

My work focuses on **EEG signal processing**, **biomedical data analysis**, **machine learning**, and **computational approaches to neuroscience**. I’m especially interested in **EEG preprocessing and decoding**, **brain signal analysis**, and **medical image analysis**.

More broadly, I’m interested in using **AI** and **quantitative methods** to study **brain signals** and **neural processes**, and to build computational tools for **neuroscience research** and **healthcare applications**.
