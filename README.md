## Selected Projects

- [EEG-Emotion-Recognition](https://github.com/Parichehr13/EEG-Emotion-Recognition)  
  EEG-based emotion recognition using deep learning with the DEAP and SEED datasets.

- [EEG-Processing](https://github.com/Parichehr13/EEG-Processing)  
  A broader EEG workflow covering preprocessing, ICA artifact removal, ERP analysis, and time-frequency methods.

- [Neural-Systems](https://github.com/Parichehr13/Neural-Systems)  
  Computational explorations of neural models, from neuron dynamics to foundational network ideas.

- [biological-system-modeling](https://github.com/Parichehr13/biological-system-modeling)  
  Mathematical and simulation-based modeling of biological systems, including membrane dynamics and Hodgkin-Huxley-style neurons.

- [Medical-Imaging-Analysis](https://github.com/Parichehr13/Medical-Imaging-Analysis)  
  Medical imaging work focused on registration, segmentation, and 3D reconstruction from MRI and DICOM data.
