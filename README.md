## 🔬 About Me

I am a Biomedical Engineering MSc student at **UNIBO** with a strong interest in the intersection of **neuroscience**, **signal processing**, and **computational modeling**. My work focuses on extracting meaningful structure from biological data through **EEG analysis**, **biosignal processing**, **neural systems modeling**, and **machine learning for biomedical applications**.

I enjoy building projects that connect theory and practice, from **brain signal decoding** and **emotion recognition** to **ECG analysis**, **medical imaging**, and **mathematical models of biological systems**.

## 📊 Selected Projects

### 🧠 [EEG-Emotion-Recognition](https://github.com/Parichehr13/EEG-Emotion-Recognition)
EEG-based emotion recognition using **CNNs** and **hyperparameter optimization**, with experiments on the **DEAP** and **SEED** datasets.

### ⚡ [EEG-Processing](https://github.com/Parichehr13/EEG-Processing)
A broad EEG workflow covering **preprocessing**, **ICA artifact removal**, **ERP analysis**, **time-frequency analysis**, and **motor-imagery decoding**.

### 📈 [eeg-bfv-coherence-analysis](https://github.com/Parichehr13/eeg-bfv-coherence-analysis)
Analysis of **EEG band-power variability** and **coherence** in relation to **cerebral blood flow velocity**, connecting electrophysiology with physiological dynamics.

### 🫀 [ECG-Signal-Processing](https://github.com/Parichehr13/ECG-Signal-Processing)
Projects on **wavelet-based ECG feature extraction** and **PQRST delineation**, with a focus on interpretable cardiovascular signal analysis.

### 🖼️ [Medical-Imaging-Analysis](https://github.com/Parichehr13/Medical-Imaging-Analysis)
Medical imaging workflows for **multimodal registration**, **level set segmentation**, and **3D reconstruction** from **DICOM/MRI** data.

### 🧬 [Neural-Systems](https://github.com/Parichehr13/Neural-Systems)
Computational explorations of **integrate-and-fire neurons**, **neural mass models**, **Hopfield networks**, and deep learning foundations.
